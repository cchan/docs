Team Information¶

We are a team from Waterloo competing at SpaceX Hyperloop Competition. We are based at University of Waterloo and most of our team members are from this University. Our goal is to innovate the field of transportation and make Hyperloop a reality.

Mission¶

  • To strongly represent Waterloo as a hub for innovation through the SpaceX Hyperloop Competition
  • To advance transportation technology
  • To teach team members technical skills and strong engineering practices
